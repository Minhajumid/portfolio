Assalamualaikum! This is my website, so I have to say something about myself. Sometimes it is hard to introduce yourself because you know yourself so well that you do not know where to start with. Let me give a try to see what kind of image you have about me through my self- description. I hope that my impression about myself and your impression about me are not so different.

Here it goes....

I am a person who is posetive about every aspect of life. There are many things I like to do, to see, and to experience. I like to read, I like to write, I like to think, I like to dream I like to talk, I like to listen. I like to see the sunrise in the morning, I like to see the moonlight at night . I like to look at the clouds in the sky with a blank mind. I like to do thought something or someone special when I cannot sleep in the middle of the night. I like to sleep late, I like to get up early; I like to be alone, I like to be surrounded by people. I like delicious food. I Like to wear colourfull 'panjabi'. I like good books and action movies. I like the land and the nature, I like people. And,obviously I like to laugh.

I am a crazy fan of 'Bangladesh Cricket Team' ♥ ♥ I always wanted to be a pilot...as I gonna fly in the sky... I also wanted to be a great programmer. Of course, I am nowhere close to these, yet. I am just someone who does some coding.

I'm not immature, I just know how to have fun! I make shoe contact before eye contact. I'm not unlucky in love, I'm just extremely good at being single! I am too hot to handle and too cold to hold. I'm slow to trust but quick to love. I wish too hard and give too much. I'm not saying I'm perfect, but I promise I'm worth it! This is a brief introduction of myself.

Do not expect too much, and keep your sense of humor.
